My Expertise

VR360 Virtual Tours

Create immersive 360-degree virtual tours for real estate, museums, tourism, and businesses. Using professional tools like 3D Vista and Panotour Pro for web and VR platform compatibility. I also specialize in photographing smaller 360 objects on a moving stand with photobox setup for detailed product showcases.

Graphic Design

Master-level expertise in Adobe Creative Suite - Photoshop, Illustrator, and Lightroom. From photo retouching and digital painting to vector design and brand identity creation.

360° Panorama Creation

High-quality 360 panorama assembly using PTGui, combined with expert photo retouching and color grading to create stunning visual experiences that captivate audiences.

Interactive Interface Design

User-friendly navigation design and interactive hotspot development, creating intuitive interfaces that enhance user engagement and provide seamless exploration experiences.

About Neven Pesic

With over 10 years of dedicated experience in graphic design and VR 360 development, I specialize in crafting immersive virtual tours that captivate and engage audiences. My expertise lies in transforming spaces into interactive experiences.

I'm passionate about pushing the boundaries of visual storytelling through innovative design and cutting-edge technology. My attention to detail and commitment to quality ensure that each project is not just a tour, but an unforgettable experience.

For capturing 360-degree content, I use professional equipment including the Insta 360 camera and Xiaomi Mi Sphere camera, ensuring high-quality panoramic photography for every project.

VR 360 tours are powerful marketing tools that allow users to explore spaces remotely, offering an engaging, interactive experience that attracts more visitors and enhances brand presence.
